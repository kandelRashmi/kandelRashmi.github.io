About Me

Hey there! I’m Rashmi - Nepal-based social entrepreneur focused on exporting Himalayan Mad honey and researching the impact of climate change on bees. With over 8 years of experience, I work closely with 80+ honey hunting communities, my pursuit is to preserve the rare and exotic honey of the Himalayas and sharing knowledge about bee habitats and environmental sustainability.

Everest organic Home

Everest Organic Home is an ethical organic products company registered with the government of Nepal. It is renowned for its authentic Himalayan products, including 100% pure organic forest wild bees honey, known as Himalayan Cliff Honey.

Shilajit and Saffron Exporter

As a silajit and saffron exporter, I specialize in sourcing and supplying high-quality Shilajit and premium saffron from the Himalayas to markets worldwide, ensuring the authenticity and integrity of these prized natural products.
